What information does our app/game obtain and how is it used ?

Account

Of course, you can also use our application directly without logging in

Data Backups

We provide two data backup methods, local storage and cloud storage. We use firebase database for cloud storage. After you log in to your account, the application will automatically encrypt and store your data in the cloud.

Analytics

Our application may collect some anonymized usage data that are essential for us to deliver our services, to understand your needs, and to improve our services. Such as app launches, taps, clicks, in-app purchases, screen visits, or other information about how users interact with our app. These data are fully anonymized and aggregated and thus not linked to any particular user. To find bugs and maximize stability, we also collect crashes and look at performance reports.

Your query or feedback
